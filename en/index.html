About me

My name is Ing. Erin Zeleňáková and I operate Erinka Taxi.

Erinka Taxi originally started as a way to provide safe rides for women. Over time it also expanded to family rides, transport for seniors, school rides for children, airport transfers, celebrations and other events.

People are different. Some want to talk during the ride, others prefer to rest in silence. I respect both.

My goal is to get you to your destination safely, calmly and with the feeling that you were in good hands.

You may also know me from Bolt, Uber or ZoberMa, where you can meet me as a driver outside pre-booked rides as well.

I look forward to driving you safely to your destination.
